Milestones

  • Looking Ahead

    I aspire to leverage my experience and skills to secure a position as a software engineer.
    This milestone will allow me to be part of a team on projects, contribute to innovative solutions within the tech industry,
    and work my way to becoming a Senior Software Engineer.

  • Software Engineer Fellowship(Headstarter)

    Currently, I am enrolled in a software engineering fellowship with Headstarter.
    This fellowship provides me with hands-on experience and mentorship, allowing me to apply and expand my coding skills in a professional environment.
    It has been instrumental in advancing my career as a software engineer.

  • Women Who Code

    After Completing my bootcamp, I discovered the group Women Who Code, which was hosting a Days Of Code event.
    I signed up and participated in the 60 Days of Code challenge, working on various projects.
    This experience further honed my skills and deepened my commitment to becoming a software engineer.

  • Bootcamp

    After working as an ERP Administrator and collaborating with various developers, I developed a strong interest in coding.
    This newfound passion led me to seek out and attend a coding bootcamp, where I could immerse myself in software development.
    The intensive training provided the skills and knowledge necessary to pursue a career in software engineering.

  • First Job in IT

    My journey into IT began in customer service, where I quickly became the go-to person
    for technological issues. Recognizing my passion and aptitude for technology,
    one of my supervisors promoted me to the IT team as an ERP Administrator.
    This opportunity marked the official start of my career in information technology.

  • International Relocation: Embracing New Opportunities

    In 2016, I relocated to the USA which expanded my professional network, exposed me to new industry practices,
    and enhanced my ability to adapt to diverse work environments.
